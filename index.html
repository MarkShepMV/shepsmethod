The S.H.E.P.S. Method℠

Five daily habits. That’s the whole system.

The medication does the heavy lifting. These five habits make sure you lose fat, not muscle — and keep the weight off when you taper.

S

Sleep

7–9 hours. Protects the lean mass the medication cannot.

H

Hydration

Half your body weight in ounces. Add electrolytes.

E

Exercise

Resistance 2–3× per week. Walk on the other days.

P

Protein

0.8 to 1.2g per pound of target body weight. Roughly 30g per meal.

S

Supplements

Six basics: multivitamin, magnesium glycinate, psyllium fiber, B12, vitamin D3, omega-3. Plus creatine 10g daily. The rest fills food gaps.
